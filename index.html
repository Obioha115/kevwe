About Me

Kevwe Solomon Ogunje, is a British trained Nigerian Delta Born Actor, Model, Producer, TV Host and Master of Ceremonies. Born 18th of August, he is a graduate of the prestigious Benson Idahosa University, with a degree in Information systems and communication, a certified NIIT Microsoft system engineer.

In a quest for higher education, he left the shores of Nigeria to the United Kingdom where he earned a Master’s Degree in Management and information technology at Salford University Manchester. Upon graduation, he decided to chase an acting career joining Nollywood London, there he got involved in successful movie projects working with top UK film directors like Nelson Spykk. Toyin Moore, Ruke Amata, Sukanmi Oduwole, Roseline Sanni Ajose, Ikenna Nwabue, including producing his own sitcom, which earned him a BEFTA award. Kevwe Solomon Ogunje, has been referred to as UK Nollywood’s most promising actor, model, TV host and entertainer.

Some of his series have been featured on ITV London and top London and top London cable network.

Upon his return to Nigeria, in 2016 he has starred in couple of movies with veteran actors, like Desmond Elliot, Kiki Omeli, Victor Olaitan, Mercy Johnson, Chioma Chukwuka Apkotha, and also Comedians Funnybone, Senator, Mc Shakara, Dan de Humours, Romeo,Josh2funny,mention a few.
